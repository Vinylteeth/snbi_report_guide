Approach Roadway Alignment (B.AP.01)
N

NOT APPLICABLEThe bridge does not carry vehicular traffic and does not have an operating speed.

G

GOODThe operating speed at the bridge is no different than the rest of the highway segment.

F

FAIRThe operating speed at the bridge is noticeably different compared to the rest of the highway segment.

P

POORThe operating speed at the bridge is substantially different compared to the rest of the highway segment.
