Preferred CV Suffixes and Naming Conventions:

  • SB, NB, EB, WB: Under southbound, northbound, eastbound, or westbound only. This is for culverts that have an opening in the median.

  • SBR, NBR, EBR, WBR: Under southbound, northbound, eastbound, or westbound ramp only.

  • ADJ: Adjacent to the road (can add NB, SB, EB, WB to designate directional travel side of the road, e.g., NBADJ would mean the culvert is under a road adjacent to the northbound lanes)

  • CR: One end is under a County Road (can add NB, SB, EB, WB to designate directional travel side of the road, e.g., NBCR would mean the culvert runs under a county road adjacent to the northbound lanes)

  • SPC: Special notes on the location can be found in the report (catch basin, only has one open end, part of a complex system, confined space, etc.)

  • Combinations are possible: SB-SBCR, NB-NBR, EBR-EBCR, WBADJ-SPC
